Instructions

Skunk Trust is a strange place. Boasting a whopping population of 200, this rural American town is forever arrested in the early 2000s. Explore uncharted regions, Discover secrets, fight enemies in intense combat, solve puzzles, and unlock extortionate treasures in this text based adventure game. The game is controlled by simpy typing commands into the text field. Commands are sent by pressing enter.
